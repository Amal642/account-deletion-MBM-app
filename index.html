Request Account Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data stored in our systems.

How to Request Deletion

  1. From within the app: Open the app → Go to Settings → Select Delete Account and follow the instructions.
  2. Without logging into the app: Send an email to aitechappsllc@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include:
    • The Phone number linked to your account
    • Any additional details to verify your identity

What Happens Next

Once we verify your request, we will:

  • Delete your Firebase Authentication account
  • Remove all personal data from our Firestore database and Storage
  • Send you a confirmation email when deletion is complete

Please allow up to 30 days for your request to be processed.

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
